Who Am I?

I'm Tom Appenzeller But you probably already knew that because my names everywhere and I'd bet my life savings you didn't stumble on this site by accident. The purpose of this site is to represent my professional development in the tech industry. I found it difficult to adequately describe myself in a five minute elevator pitch so I decided to create this site.
